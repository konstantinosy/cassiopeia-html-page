The Green Monster!

What's the fuzz about it?

Remnants of a supernova. Such a huge explosion that created amazing artifacts.

Some quick facts about the Green Monster! It's not that scary.

  • The green outlines of the supernova signify radioactive nickel.
  • The image was composed from many different telescopes to create something beautiful!
  • Telescope data include:
    • James Webb
    • Chandra
    • Spitzer
    • Hubble
    • NuSTAR
